ACP Whitepaper

Why ACP

Most decisions today are arbitrary, non-repeatable, and hard to evaluate. ACP exists to turn decisions into structured, trackable systems.

What is ACP

ACP (Adaptive Choice Protocol) is a framework that defines how to evaluate options, standardizes selection criteria, records decision outcomes, and enables continuous improvement.

How it works

  • Specification → define rules
  • Evaluation → score candidates
  • Selection → choose optimal option
  • Snapshot → record decisions
  • Iteration → refine over time
